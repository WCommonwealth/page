About Us

The W Commonwealth, standing for Vlad Victor Commonwealth, is a private organization dedicated to crypto, tech, and privacy. Founded by two visionaries who, at 11 years old in 2021, invented the Walar language—a unique system for secret communication. Our slogan: “Speak through code”.
